Tesla's steering wheel scroll wheels and buttons control crucial functions like volume, Autopilot speed, and voice commands. When these become unresponsive or erratic, it affects your driving experience. This guide covers cleaning and troubleshooting these essential controls.

## Common Steering Wheel Button Problems

Identify your issue:

- **Scroll wheel doesn't scroll** - No response when rotating
- **Scroll wheel clicks don't register** - Press not recognized
- **Erratic behavior** - Scrolls in wrong direction or jumps
- **Button presses ignored** - Left/right buttons unresponsive
- **Intermittent response** - Works sometimes, not others

## Understanding the Controls

### Left Scroll Wheel
- **Scroll:** Volume up/down
- **Press:** Voice commands
- **Left/Right buttons:** Previous/Next track

### Right Scroll Wheel
- **Scroll:** Autopilot follow distance
- **Press:** Confirm selection (varies by context)
- **Left/Right buttons:** Autopilot speed adjust

### Both Wheels Together
- **Press both 10 seconds:** System reboot

## Quick Fixes First

### System Reboot

Sometimes buttons become unresponsive due to software glitches:

1. Hold both scroll wheels for 10 seconds
2. Screen goes dark, then Tesla logo appears
3. Wait for full restart (2-3 minutes)
4. Test buttons again

### Check for Software Updates

Updates sometimes fix button responsiveness:

1. Go to **Controls > Software**
2. Install any available updates
3. Test after update completes

## Cleaning the Scroll Wheels

Most scroll wheel problems are caused by dirt, oils, and debris accumulation.

### Basic Cleaning

<div class="tip-box">
<strong>What You'll Need:</strong>
<ul>
<li>99% Isopropyl alcohol</li>
<li>Microfiber cloth</li>
<li>Compressed air</li>
<li>Plastic dental picks (optional)</li>
</ul>
</div>

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Turn off the car** (Controls > Safety > Power Off)
2. **Wait 3 minutes** for systems to power down
3. **Spray compressed air** around the scroll wheel edges
4. **Dampen microfiber cloth** with isopropyl alcohol (not soaking wet)
5. **Wipe around the scroll wheel** circumference
6. **Rotate the wheel** while wiping to clean all surfaces
7. **Use plastic pick** to clean around the edges gently
8. **Let dry** for 2-3 minutes
9. **Wake the car** and test

### Deep Cleaning Technique

For severely stuck scroll wheels:

1. Apply isopropyl alcohol to a cotton swab
2. Rotate the wheel while running the swab around edges
3. Work the alcohol into the gaps
4. Rotate wheel repeatedly while cleaning
5. Use compressed air to blow out debris
6. Repeat 2-3 times if needed

<div class="warning-box">
<strong>Warning:</strong> Never use water or household cleaners on the scroll wheels. Only use 99% isopropyl alcohol which evaporates quickly and won't damage electronics.
</div>

## Button Cleaning

### Side Buttons

The left/right buttons next to each scroll wheel:

1. Use compressed air to blow out debris
2. Wipe with alcohol-dampened cloth
3. Press buttons repeatedly while cleaning
4. Check for improved response

### Common Contamination Sources

- Hand lotions and oils
- Food residue
- Dust and lint
- Dried liquid spills

## Scroll Wheel Calibration

Tesla scroll wheels don't require manual calibration, but you can reset them:

### Soft Reset

1. Perform system reboot (both wheels 10 seconds)
2. After restart, test each function
3. Often resolves erratic behavior

### Full Power Cycle

1. Go to **Controls > Safety > Power Off**
2. Exit the car and close doors
3. Wait 5 minutes
4. Open door and press brake to wake
5. Test scroll wheel functions

## Hardware Issues

### Signs of Hardware Failure

- Cleaning doesn't improve response
- Physical damage visible
- Clicking/grinding feeling when scrolling
- Complete lack of response in any function

### Scroll Wheel Replacement

If cleaning doesn't help:

**DIY Replacement:**
- Aftermarket scroll wheels available online
- Requires removing airbag (caution!)
- Moderate to difficult repair
- Cost: $50-150 for parts

**Tesla Service:**
- Professional replacement
- Warranty-safe
- Cost: $300-500 installed
- Recommended for most owners

<div class="warning-box">
<strong>Important:</strong> Working near airbag components is dangerous. If you're not experienced with automotive electrical systems, have Tesla service perform scroll wheel replacement.
</div>

## Preventive Maintenance

Keep scroll wheels working smoothly:

- **Clean monthly** with isopropyl alcohol
- **Avoid eating** while driving
- **Wash hands** before driving when possible
- **Use compressed air** weekly during dusty seasons

## When to Contact Service

Visit Tesla service if:

- Cleaning doesn't restore function
- Multiple buttons fail simultaneously
- Physical damage to controls
- Warranty period still active
- Not comfortable working near airbag

### Service Appointment Tips

- Document which buttons/functions are affected
- Note when problems started
- Mention any cleaning attempts
- Check warranty coverage first
